Teams:
PK: TEAM#<teamId>
SK: METADATA#<teamId>

Columns:
PK: COLUMN#<columnId>
SK: METADATA#<columnId>

Sprints:
PK: TEAM#<teamId>
SK: SPRINT#<sprintId>

Cards:
PK: TEAM#<teamId>
SK: SPRINT#<sprintId>#COLUMN#<columnId>#CARD#<cardId>

Users (separate table):
PK: USER#<userId>
SK: METADATA#<userId>

Cards (main table):
PK: TEAM#<teamId>
SK: SPRINT#<sprintId>#COLUMN#<columnId>#CARD#<cardId>
// Additional attributes:
createdBy: <userId>
createdAt: <timestamp>

Votes (main table):
PK: TEAM#<teamId>
SK: SPRINT#<sprintId>#COLUMN#<columnId>#CARD#<cardId>#VOTE#<userId>
// Additional attributes:
votedBy: <userId>
votedAt: <timestamp>

Comments (main table):
PK: TEAM#<teamId>
SK: SPRINT#<sprintId>#COLUMN#<columnId>#CARD#<cardId>#COMMENT#<timestamp>#<commentId>
// Additional attributes:
commentedBy: <userId>
content: <string>
